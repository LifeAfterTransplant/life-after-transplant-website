Transplant Education Program

Life After Transplant

A nurse-developed mobile education application designed to reinforce discharge teaching for adult liver and kidney transplant recipients.

Contact App Support Read Privacy Policy

Education for the transition home

Life After Transplant provides short educational videos, interactive learning activities, frequently asked questions, and safety guidance intended to supplement education provided by the transplant team.

Focused education

Review medication safety, infection prevention, nutrition and food safety, and blood sugar and insulin basics.

Private by design

The app does not collect, transmit, or store protected health information or other personal information.

Controlled access

The app is free and access-code protected. Access is provided by participating transplant programs.

Educational use only. Life After Transplant does not replace medical advice, emergency care, or instructions from a transplant team. Always follow the instructions provided by your transplant team.
