About the app

Nurse-developed education

Created to reinforce education provided by transplant clinicians and help adult recipients review important information after discharge.

Free and access-code protected

The application is free and access is provided by participating transplant programs.

Educational use only. Life After Transplant does not replace medical advice, emergency care, or instructions from a transplant team. Always follow the instructions provided by your transplant team.
