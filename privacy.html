Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Life After Transplant respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the application does and does not collect. Life After Transplant is developed and operated by Amanda Ek.

Information We Do Not Collect

The application does not collect, transmit, or store protected health information (PHI) or personal information. It does not request or collect names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses, medication lists, laboratory results, medical records, photographs, biometric information, or other health information. The application does not require users to create an account.

Information Stored on Your Device

The application stores only limited information locally on your device, including whether access has been authorized and your progress through educational videos and activities. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Life After Transplant, Amanda Ek, Westchester Medical Center, New York Medical College, or any other organization. The application does not synchronize this information across devices or back it up to Life After Transplant servers.

Analytics, Advertising, and Data Sharing

The application does not use advertising services, third-party analytics services, or tracking technologies. Life After Transplant does not sell, rent, or share personal information because the application does not collect personal information.

Access Codes

Access codes are used only to limit access to the educational materials. The application checks entered codes against codes stored within the application. Entered access codes are not transmitted to a server and are not associated with a user's identity or medical information.

Children's Privacy

The application is intended for adult transplant recipients and their caregivers. It is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to maintain the security and integrity of the application. Users are responsible for maintaining appropriate security protections on their own devices, including device passwords and software updates. No electronic system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the application or its data practices change. The current version will be available within the application and on this website.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to Life After Transplant at amanda@life-after-transplant.com.
